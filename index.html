Marketing Strategy Pillars

How a B2B logistics powerhouse captures market share and generates leads via paid and organic Facebook loops.

1. Content Pillar: B2B Operational Trust

  • Warehouse Spotlights: High-definition tours showcasing advanced vertical storage, clean ambient layouts, and WMS inventory tracking systems.
  • Cold Chain Compliance: Short videos detailing how Genex maintains strict temperature zones for pharmaceutical shipments.
  • Project Cargo (Heavy Lift): Visual coverage of transport operations involving oversize turbines, plant boilers, and manufacturing equipment.

2. Content Pillar: Supply Chain Insight

  • FTWZ Advantage Guides: Explainers on how Free Trade Warehousing Zones save customs duties and cash flows for multinational exporters.
  • Logistics Tech Reels: Showcasing automated parcel sorting, active GPS monitoring, and client dashboard analytics interfaces.
  • Industry Case Studies: "How Genex optimized retail logistics to cut transit delays by 22% for an e-commerce partner."
